Frequently Asked Questions

Buying FAQs

Our linen-silk and cotton-linen blends are pre-washed and treated to ensure a soft, comfortable drape right out of the box, unlike stiff pure linen fabrics.

Our standard Linen-Silk sarees feature a balanced blend of 60% premium organic linen and 40% soft art silk, giving you natural breathability with a polished silk finish.

Our linen collections are pre-shrunk during printing. We recommend dry cleaning or gentle hand washing to maintain the natural fiber texture.

We support Bank Transfers (IMPS, NEFT, RTGS), UPI, GPay, and cash payment directly at our Surat wholesale counter. We do not support credit terms for new retail accounts.

Shipping charges are calculated based on cargo weight and target delivery pincode. We use competitive transport rates via Surat's primary logistics channels and charge shipping at actual cost.
