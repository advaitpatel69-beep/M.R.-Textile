Who We Are

A wholesale partner rooted in Surat's textile craft

Operating out of Manish Market in Surat — one of India's largest textile trading hubs — M.R. Textile (M.R. Sarees) supplies retailers, boutique owners and event suppliers with sarees across ten curated categories, from everyday cottons to bridal Banarasi silks.

We work directly with weaving clusters to keep our range wide and our stock moving, so shop owners can restock quickly without compromising on variety or finish.

Handpicked Fabric

Every category is reviewed for weave, drape and finish before it reaches our floor.

Bulk-Ready Stock

Wholesale quantities held across our full range, ready to dispatch to your store.

Direct Relationships

Long-standing ties with weavers help us keep pricing honest for wholesale buyers.
