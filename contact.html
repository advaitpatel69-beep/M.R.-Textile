Who We Are

A trusted saree manufacturer & wholesaler from the heart of Surat

M.R. Textile (M.R. Sarees) is a premier saree manufacturer and wholesaler based at Manish Market, Ring Road, Surat — one of India’s most celebrated textile trading hubs. We supply retailers, boutiques, and wholesale buyers across India with an extensive range of premium-quality sarees.

Our collection spans over ten curated categories — from everyday cottons and printed sarees to exquisite Banarasi silks, designer creations, and grand bridal wear — all crafted with a strong focus on quality, craftsmanship, competitive wholesale pricing, and reliable customer service. We work directly with weaving clusters to keep our range wide and our stock moving.

Premium Quality
